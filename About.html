Welcome to Splash Fitness
Where Strength Meets Lifestyle

At Splash Fitness, we're more than just a gym — we're a vibrant community fueled by passion, performance, and personal growth. From first-timers to seasoned athletes, our space is built to support every step of your fitness journey.

Meet our Inspiring Executives

John Warik

Hi, I’m John Warick, Co-CEO of Splash Fitness. Fitness has always been more than just a routine for me—it’s a way of life. My journey started with a passion for health and a belief that everyone deserves access to a space where they feel motivated, supported, and inspired to grow stronger—physically and mentally.

At Splash Fitness, my role is to ensure we’re always pushing boundaries—whether it’s through state-of-the-art equipment, professional training programs, or creating a community that truly feels like home. I’m here to make sure your fitness journey is rewarding, safe, and empowering. We’re not just building bodies—we’re building confidence, resilience, and long-term well-being.

Elika John

Hello! I’m Elika John, and I’m proud to serve as Co-CEO of Splash Fitness. I’ve spent years studying the science of wellness and working closely with individuals from all walks of life, and I’ve seen firsthand how the right environment can spark incredible change.

At Splash Fitness, my mission is to make fitness inclusive, enjoyable, and sustainable. Whether you’re just starting out or you’re a long-time athlete, I want you to feel welcome here. We’re not just about lifting weights—we’re about lifting each other up. Together with John, I’m committed to making Splash Fitness a place where your goals matter and your journey is celebrated every step of the way.
